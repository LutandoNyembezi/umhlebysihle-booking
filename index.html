At UmhleBySihle, we believe that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary. Founded by Sihle, a passionate beauty artist with over a decade of experience, our salon is a sanctuary where artistry meets luxury.

We specialise in transformative experiences — from flawless makeup that captures your essence to stunning wigs and intricate braiding that tells your story. Every appointment is personalised, every result is perfection.

Our Services

💄

Makeup Artistry

From natural glam to full editorial looks. Wedding makeup, special occasions, photoshoots, and everyday glam — we craft the perfect look for every face.

👑

Wig Installation

Professional wig customisation, fitting and installation. Lace fronts, full lace, closures and more. Seamless, natural and utterly stunning results.

✨

Braiding

Box braids, knotless braids, cornrows, goddess braids and more. Protective styling that's both gorgeous and kind to your natural hair.

Nail Art

Acrylic nails, gel manicures, nail art and extensions. Complete your look from fingertip to fabulous.

🌿

Hair Treatments

Deep conditioning, scalp treatments and hair growth treatments. We care for your natural crown with premium products.

Eyebrow Shaping

Threading, waxing, tinting and lamination. Frame your face beautifully with perfectly sculpted brows.
